Pentair Residential Pool Pump Guide

SuperFlo

Horsepower: 1.0 – 2.0 HP (up to 2.5 HP up-rated)

Plumbing Size: 1.5″ ports

Replacement Parts:

  • Impeller: 355067 (1 HP), 355074 (1.5 HP), 355093 (2 HP/2.5 HP up-rated)
  • Diffuser: 355617 (0.5–1.0 HP) / 355618 (1.5–2.0 HP)
  • Shaft Seal: 354545 (OEM; Generic: PS-200)
  • Seal Plate: 356012
  • Motor: 355022S (1 HP), 355024S (1.5 HP), 355026S (2 HP)
  • VS Drive: N/A

SuperFlo VS

Horsepower: 1.5 HP (variable speed)

Plumbing Size: 1.5″ ports

Replacement Parts:

  • Impeller: 355074
  • Diffuser: 355618
  • Shaft Seal: 354545 (OEM; Generic: PS-200)
  • Seal Plate: 356012
  • Motor: 353134S (permanent magnet VS motor)
  • VS Drive: 353127 (drive & cover kit)

WhisperFlo

Horsepower: 1.0 – 2.5 HP (up to 3.0 HP full-rated)

Plumbing Size: 2″ x 2″ ports (with union kit option for 2.5″)

Replacement Parts:

  • Impeller: 073128 (1 HP), 073129 (1.5 HP), 073130 (2 HP/2.5 HP up-rated)
  • Diffuser: 072927
  • Shaft Seal: 071734 (OEM; Generic: PS-1000)
  • Seal Plate: 074564
  • Motor: 355022S (1 HP), 355024S (1.5 HP), 355026S (2 HP)
  • VS Drive: N/A
