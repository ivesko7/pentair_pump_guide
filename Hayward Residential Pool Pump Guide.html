Hayward Residential Pool Pump Identification Guide

Super Pump

Horsepower: 1.0 - 2.0 HP

Plumbing Size: 1.5"

Replacement Parts:

  • Impeller: SPX2607C, SPX2610C, SPX2615C
  • Diffuser: SPX2600B
  • Shaft Seal: SPX1600Z2 (Generic: PS-200)
  • Seal Plate: SPX2600E5
  • Motor: SPX1607Z1M, SPX1610Z1M
  • VS Drive: N/A

Super II

Horsepower: 1.0 - 2.0 HP

Plumbing Size: 2"

Replacement Parts:

  • Impeller: SPX3010C, SPX3015C, SPX3020C
  • Diffuser: SPX3000BN
  • Shaft Seal: SPX3020SA (Generic: PS-201)
  • Seal Plate: SPX3000E
  • Motor: SPX3010Z1, SPX3015Z1
  • VS Drive: N/A

NorthStar

Horsepower: 1.0 - 3.0 HP

Plumbing Size: 2.0" - 2.5"

Replacement Parts:

  • Diffuser: SPX4000B

  • Seal Plate: SPX4000E
  • Motor: SPX1607Z1M, SPX1610Z1M, SPX1620Z1M, SPX1630Z1M
  • VS Drive: N/A
